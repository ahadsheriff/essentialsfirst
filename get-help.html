The Kits Program

  • The program is led by young volunteers
  • Kits in various sizes are filled with up to 10 critical hygiene products and resource information.
  • These kits are gifted to people experiencing homelessness and those near homeless, refugees and very low income people and families.
  • We partner with organizations that serve children, adults and elderly residents who are less likely to access the food bank system.

Just Essentials Program

Focuses on the procurement of large quantities (pallets) of hygiene supplies that are distributed through existing food bank distribution systems that serve more than 600 food banks and pantries across WA State.

Essentials First aims to raise awareness of homelessness while doing something tangible that all members of the community can participate in.
