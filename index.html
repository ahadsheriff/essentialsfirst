Help Sustain Our Efforts

Essentials First empowers dignity daily through our procurement and distribution network. We strengthen community-wide wellness by providing equitable access to vital personal and household hygiene products.

Our gift of free-of-cost hygiene products to our most vulnerable neighbors results in:

  • Reduced anxiety, depression and stress
  • Reduction in the spread of illness and serious disease
  • Less isolation among our neighbors in crisis
  • Return of dignity and self confidence to those who have gone without
  • Greater public health data collaboration

Peaceful Projects


Afghan Refugee Support Fund

Several Afghan refugee families are already here in the Puget Sound area. More will be arriving here through the resettlement process. Essentials First has been working with local refugee families and is mobilizing to support these newly arriving Afghan families.

Support Afghan Refugees


Our Covid-19 Response

We have recently started our Sponsor a Family program as an increasing number of households are experiencing financial hardship because of COVID -19. This program will provide immediate food and rental assistance for families until they can get back on their feet. Sponsors can come together to adopt a family to help us prevent evictions and homelessness.

Sponsor A Family


Give A Gift To A Child In Need

Every child deserves love. Open your heart to support orphans and children in need. Essentials First will use funds raised to meet the emotional, intellectual, physical, social and spiritual needs of these children to ensure that they can live peaceful lives.
