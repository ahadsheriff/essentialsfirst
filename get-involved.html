Peaceful Projects

Afghan Refugee Support Fund

Several Afghan refugee families are already here in the Puget Sound area. More will be arriving here through the resettlement process. Kits for Peace has been working with local refugee families and is mobilizing to support these newly arriving Afghan families.

Support Afghan Refugees



Our Covid-19 Response

We have recently started our Sponsor a Family program as an increasing number of households are experiencing financial hardship because of COVID -19. This program will provide immediate food and rental assistance for families until they can get back on their feet. Sponsors can come together to adopt a family to help us prevent evictions and homelessness.

Sponsor A Family



Give A Gift To A Child In Need

Every child deserves love. Open your heart to support orphans and children in need. Kits for Peace will use funds raised to meet the emotional, intellectual, physical, social and spiritual needs of these children to ensure that they can live peaceful lives.
